Our Story


Our company was founded by two coffee and cat lovers with a common goal of sharing quality and fresh coffee to all. All of our coffee beans are ethically sourced in Vietnam and Thailand. We are passionate about coffee and our mission is to bring you a sense of comfort, one cup at a time. A portion of our profit will go towards our local cat shelters.
